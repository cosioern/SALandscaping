# Landscaping Site
Commercial Site advertising the services of Santa Ana Quality Lawn Care.

## Description
This project is a brochure site for the small business Santa Ana Quality Lawn Care. </br>
The site will will demonstrate the services the offered by the company, images of previous work, a mission statement, and contact information.

This small business has provided lawncare services to southeastern Wiconsin for over twenty years. The Wisconsin towns they operate in are Salem Lakes, Twin Lakes, Paddock Lake, Kenosha and Bristol, while the IL towns are Antioch and Lake Villa.
</br>Here is their facebook page: [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/santaanalandscaping/)

This project is in its development stage and thus makes use of placeholder images and text for design and structural purpose. </br>
*Many of these placeholders are meant to be humorous, and not indicative of the final product.*
